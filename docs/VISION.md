# Speechef — Product Vision

> **"Communicate your way to the top."**

---

## What is Speechef?

Speechef is an all-in-one platform for mastering English communication. It combines structured learning, vocabulary practice, AI-powered coaching, peer community, and career tools — all under one roof.

| Audience | What they come for |
|---|---|
| **Learners** | Build vocabulary, practice speaking, get AI feedback, track progress |
| **Professionals** | Prepare for IELTS / TOEFL / PTE, ace job interviews, improve writing |
| **Mentors** | Share expertise, build a coaching profile, offer 1-on-1 sessions |
| **Job seekers** | Find communication and speaking roles, get resume feedback |

---

## The Problem We Solve

Most people know they need to communicate better — but they have no structured, affordable, self-paced way to practice. Toastmasters requires physical attendance. YouTube is passive. Coaches are expensive. Test prep apps lack conversation practice.

Speechef closes every gap: daily vocabulary games build habit, AI coaches simulate real conversations, structured tracks prepare for exams, and a peer community makes it social.

## V1 — What Shipped

### Learn — Recipe Book
A curated knowledge base organized by topic (Grammar, Pronunciation, Fluency, Vocabulary, IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, OET, CELPIP, etc.). Articles are bookmarkable, completable, and tracked with a personal Speechef Meter showing overall progress.

### Practice — Word Games
Six daily vocabulary drills that build habit through play:
- **Vocabulary Blitz** — 60-second sprint, as many questions as possible
- **Guess the Word** — multiple-choice vocabulary
- **Memory Match** — flip cards to pair words with meanings
- **Word Scramble** — unscramble letters under pressure
- **Sentence Builder** — construct sentences with target words, AI-graded
- **Pronunciation Challenge** — speak aloud, get real-time feedback

### Practice — AI Roleplay
Four real-time conversational scenarios powered by GPT-4o-mini:
- **Job Interview** — tough behavioral and situational questions
- **Presentation Pitch** — pitch to an AI audience, handle follow-up questions
- **Debate** — argue a position against an AI opponent
- **Small Talk** — everyday social and networking scenarios

### Practice — Interview Simulation
Text-based mock interviews with configurable mode (behavioral / technical / HR / mixed) and difficulty. Per-answer scoring, ideal answer reveal, and a final report with strengths and improvements.

### Practice — Test Prep
Structured preparation tracks for five major English exams:
IELTS Academic · TOEFL iBT · PTE Academic · OET · CELPIP

### Practice — AI Writing Coach
Submit any essay, email, cover letter, or IELTS task. GPT-4o returns: band score, grammar errors with suggestions, vocabulary rating, structure and coherence feedback, improved excerpt, and a coaching narrative.

### Practice — Resume Analyzer
Paste a resume and target role. GPT-4o returns: ATS compatibility score, language score, strong phrases to keep, weak phrases to rephrase, missing keywords, and a coaching summary.

### Vocabulary Hub
- **Academic Vocabulary Tracker** — 550+ graded academic words with progress tracking
- **My Saved Words** — personal collection: save any word while reading or browsing

### Dashboard
Personal command centre: journey level card (Starter → Learner → Skilled → Expert), Speechef Communication Score, 7-day streak heatmap, skill breakdown bars, weekly goal tracker, score trend chart, and a leaderboard.

### Speech Analysis
Record or upload a speech clip. AI returns an overall Communication Score with sub-scores for fluency, vocabulary, and pace (WPM). Shareable scorecard with OG image.

### Mentors
Browse a curated directory of communication coaches. View profiles, specialties, and session details. Book directly via the platform. Follow mentors to stay updated. Apply to become a mentor.

### Community
A peer Q&A forum with category-filtered threads (Grammar · Vocabulary · Pronunciation · Writing · Test Prep · General). Post questions, reply, upvote, and accept the best answer.

### Jobs
A curated job board for speaking and communication roles — MC gigs, corporate trainers, event speakers, English instructors. Track applications in-platform.

### PWA
Installable as a home-screen app on iOS and Android. Service worker provides offline fallback and caches static assets for fast reload.

---

## Product Principles

1. **Every feature is functional** — no placeholder buttons, no "coming soon" on shipped features
2. **Progress is visible** — streaks, scores, and completion percentages motivate consistency
3. **Simple beats clever** — a great vocabulary game beats a mediocre AI feature every time
4. **AI where it adds real value** — roleplay, writing feedback, scoring; not gimmicks
5. **Community compounds value** — the platform gets better as more people use it
