# Speechef — Product Vision

> **"Communicate your way to the top."**

---

## What is Speechef?

Speechef is an all-in-one platform for mastering communication and public speaking.
It serves two audiences simultaneously:

| Audience | What they come for |
|---|---|
| **Learners** | Practice vocabulary, improve speech, get AI and peer feedback |
| **Organizers** | Find and hire expert speakers for events |

---

## The Problem We Solve

Most people know they need to communicate better — but they have no structured way to practice. Toastmasters requires physical attendance. YouTube is passive. Coaches are expensive. And no single platform combines vocabulary training, speech practice, peer review, and expert access.

Speechef closes that gap.

---

## Core Pillars

### 1. Learn
A curated knowledge base: articles, guides, and techniques for public speaking and communication — organized by category, searchable, and completable.

### 2. Practice
Interactive vocabulary and language games that build the foundation of confident speech:
- **Guess the Word** — vocabulary through multiple choice
- **Memory Match** — word-meaning association
- **Word Scramble** — letter unscrambling under pressure
- Future: pronunciation drills, filler-word detection, pacing exercises

### 3. Compete (Roadmap)
Daily challenges, streaks, and a global leaderboard. Gamification that turns practice into a habit.

### 4. Record & Review (Roadmap)
Upload a speech video. Get peer reviews from the community. Earn review credits by reviewing others. AI scoring on pace, filler words, and clarity.

### 5. Experts (Roadmap)
A marketplace of vetted communication coaches and public speakers available for 1:1 sessions.

### 6. Jobs
A dedicated job board for speaking and communication roles — MC gigs, corporate trainers, event speakers.

---

## Target Users

| Persona | Description |
|---|---|
| **Student** | Preparing for debates, interviews, or presentations |
| **Professional** | Building career communication skills, preparing for TEDx |
| **Event Organizer** | Hiring speakers for corporate events, conferences |
| **Coach / Expert** | Offering services and growing a client base |

---

## What Success Looks Like

- A learner completes a 7-day streak of vocabulary games
- A user uploads their first speech video and gets 3 peer reviews
- An event organizer books an expert speaker through the platform
- A professional lands a job through the Speechef jobs board
- Daily active users return because their streak is at stake

---

## Product Principles

1. **Every feature must be functional** — no placeholder buttons in production
2. **Mobile-first** — the majority of practice happens on phones
3. **Progress is visible** — streaks, scores, and stats motivate consistency
4. **Community compounds value** — peer review makes the platform better as it grows
5. **Simple beats clever** — a great word game beats a mediocre AI feature every time
